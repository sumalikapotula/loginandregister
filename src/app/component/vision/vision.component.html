Vision

To provide affordable quality education, while equipping students with knowledge and skills
in their chosen stream, inculcate values, identify hidden talents, provide opportunities
for students to realize theirfullpotential and thus shape them into future leaders,
entrepreneurs and above all good human beings.

Mission

To strive for quality education in keeping with the motto of the college,
“Excellence in Education” and prepare young minds
for imbibing knowledge, skills and sensitivity.
