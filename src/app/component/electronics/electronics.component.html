ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was established in the year 2001 for imparting a state-of the- art and
quality education, training and research in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering and allied areas. The
department offers a Bachelor’s program with an initial intake of 60 students in the year 2001, subsequently increased to 300
students per year from the academic year 2015 and Master’s programmes in VLSI System Design and Embedded Systems
with an intake of 18 students each.

Well Equipped labs and the infrastructure are regularly upgraded not only to serve the purpose of the curriculum prescribed but
also facilitate to carry out innovative projects to challenge the fast-changing needs of the society besides undertaking R&D
activities in various fields. We provide a very informal atmosphere between faculty and students. ECE department has always
been known for creating an ambience where scholarship flourishes. Faculty members of our department are not only engaged in
trending technology research but are also very passionate about teaching and learning process.

Faculty and students of ECE are encouraged to do NPTEL certifications in various subjects and few of them got Top 1%, Top
2% and Top 5% and many got ELITE.

VISION OF THE DEPARTMENT

To be an acknowledged Leader in providing quality education, training and research in area of Electronics and Communication
Engineering to meet the industrial and Societal needs

MISSION OF THE DEPARTMENT

The department of electronics engineering strives.
To facilitate students with a state of the art infrastructure learning environment and value based education to improve technical
knowledge and skills for continuous learning process.
To impart high quality education with well qualified faculty to enable students meet the challenges of the industry at global level.
To promote innovation and active industry institute interaction by facilitating the students to improve their leadership and
entrepreneurship skills with ethical values.
